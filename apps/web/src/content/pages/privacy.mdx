## Consent

By accessing or using Leap AI, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of information as outlined. Our SDKs and APIs are designed with "Privacy by Design" principles to ensure minimal data exposure.

---

## Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, secure, and improve our AI services.

| Category | Examples | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **Account Information** | Email, GitHub ID, API keys | Account creation, authentication, billing |
| **API Usage Data** | Request metadata, token counts, latency metrics | Scaling, usage monitoring, infrastructure optimization |
| **Technical Data** | OS version (.NET framework), SDK version, IP address | Debugging SDK compatibility, performance tracking |
| **Communication** | Support tickets, feedback, feature requests | Improving developer experience |

---

## AI Data & Model Processing

When you use the Leap AI SDK to interact with various providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google):

- **Prompt Data**: We do not store the content of your prompts or AI responses unless you explicitly enable logging or debugging features in your pipeline configuration.
- **Provider Passthrough**: Data sent to third-party providers via Leap AI is subject to those providers' respective privacy policies.
- **Training**: By default, Leap AI **does not** use your prompt data or generated weights to train our own models.

- Personalizing documentation and developer dashboard experience

- Complying with legal requirements
